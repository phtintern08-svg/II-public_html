Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by navigating to the "New Order" page and filling out the required details including product type, quantity, and delivery information.

What is the typical delivery time?

Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days after the order is confirmed and payment is received.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After that, please contact support for assistance.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order from the "My Orders" page. Click on any order to see its current status and tracking information.
