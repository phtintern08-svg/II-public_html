FAQs

Common questions

How do I update my bank details?
Go to Settings > Bank Details and update your information. Changes are verified within 24 hours.
When will I receive my payout?
Payouts are processed on the 1st and 15th of every month for completed orders.
How to upload production photos?
Go to In Production, select an order, and use the upload section to add photos.
What is the commission rate?
The platform commission is 15% on all orders. This is deducted before payout.
